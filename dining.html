Taste the Island

Fish, Fish, and More Fish

The island is home to five exceptional restaurants, each specializing in the local culinary traditions of fish and rice. These establishments take pride in using the freshest local catches and the finest rice, prepared with a blend of time-honored techniques and modern flair. From perfectly grilled fish to fragrant rice dishes, these restaurants offer an authentic taste of the island's heritage. Whether you're seeking a laid-back meal or a refined dining experience, our island's fish and rice restaurants provide a truly memorable culinary journey.

Pan Asian

The island boasts two exquisite dining establishments, each specializing in the vibrant flavors of Pan-Asian cuisine. At these restaurants, guests can indulge in a culinary journey that spans the diverse tastes of Asia, from the aromatic spices of Thai curries to the savory delights of Japanese sushi. Whether you're in the mood for a casual meal or an elegant dining experience, our island's restaurants offer a perfect blend of tradition and innovation in every dish.

American

The island features three distinguished restaurants that specialize in American cuisine. These dining spots offer a delightful array of classic American dishes, from juicy burgers and tender steaks to comforting mac and cheese and fresh salads. Each restaurant brings its own unique twist to these beloved favorites, ensuring a memorable dining experience. Whether you're in the mood for a casual meal or a more upscale dining affair, our island's American cuisine restaurants cater to every palate with their delicious and hearty offerings.

Markets

Taniti boasts a variety of shopping options to meet all your grocery needs. The island is home to two well-stocked supermarkets, offering a wide range of products from fresh produce to household essentials. In addition, there are two smaller grocery stores that provide a more personalized shopping experience, perfect for quick trips and specialty items. For those late-night or early-morning needs, Taniti also features a convenient 24-hour convenience store, ensuring you have access to necessities around the clock. With these diverse options, you'll find everything you need right at your fingertips.
